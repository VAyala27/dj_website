Meet John Doe

I’m John Doe or DJ Alias, a music producer and live DJ with a passion for deep bass, hard drops, and building unforgettable nights. I’ve played everything from packed-out clubs to intimate underground sets, always bringing energy, rhythm, and a touch of chaos.

With years of experience in house, techno, EDM, and trap, I don’t just play tracks — I create sonic journeys. Whether you're booking for a club, a private party, or a festival, I bring the vibe.
